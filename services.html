pulmonary

If someone has pulmonary disease, this means they have a lung disease, which may affect their ability to breathe well.

Pulmonary function tests (PFT) are performed to determine if an individual is experiencing problems with their lungs. These tests measure:

  • Chest X-ray
  • Chest computerized tomography (CT) scan
  • Pulse oximetry
  • Pulse oximetry
  • Arterial blood gas test
  • B-type natriuretic peptide (BNP) blood test
  • Other blood tests
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG or EKG)
  • Echocardiogram
  • Cardiac catheterization and coronary angiogram
  • Ultrasound of the lungs
