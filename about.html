About Us

"At SGMC, we strive to be your trusted partners in health. We are committed to providing you with the necessary support throughtout your recovery journey towards achieving and maintaining optimal health."
Mission

To provide globally exceptional Health Care Services

Vision

A Healthy Aklan

Our Goals:

* To grow & improve the delivery of safe, high quality care.

* To connect the care provided to our community.

* To connect with our patients, our partners & each other with transparency & purpose.

* To be socially resposibly & using resources sustainably.

* To valuing & empowering our people.

Our Objectives:

* To deliver the best care for all our patients.

* To improved health outcomes for our community.

* To reduced waiting for patients and staff.

* To provide services where they best meet care needs.

* To lead translational and health service research.

Our Values:

* Service Excellence. We commit to our standards of excellence & dedicated to exceeding the expectations of those we serve.

* Guaranted Quality. We strive to exceed the of all of our customers by committing to deliver quality & value in every aspect of our work. We will enhance quality for all of our patients by educating them about their health & empowering them to be partners in their care.

* Mutual Compassion. We provide care with dignity concern, kindness and a respect for the diversity of our community.

* Cooperative Teamwork. We foster an ambiance of trust, collegiality, collaboration & openness.
